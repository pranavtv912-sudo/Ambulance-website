Our Foundation

Who We Are & What Drives Us

Sri Manju Ambulance Service was built on a singular belief: that no patient in a medical emergency should be left waiting. Our founders — experienced paramedics and healthcare administrators — witnessed firsthand the critical gap between a patient's need and the availability of reliable, equipped medical transport.

We stepped in to fill that gap. Today, Sri Manju operates a premium fleet of ICU, BLS, and Freezer Box ambulances strategically positioned across Hyderabad, providing 24/7 emergency services all over India.

We are not just a transport service — we are the bridge between a medical crisis and a hospital bed. We specialize in Critical ICU Ventilator transfers, continuous Oxygen support with A/c & Non-A/c vehicles, and Freezer Box services. Every vehicle is fully equipped, every crew member is certified, and every dispatch is treated as the highest priority.

Mission & Vision

The principles that guide every decision we make, every dispatch we send, and every patient we serve.

Our Mission

Delivering High-Speed Compassionate Critical Care

To provide every patient in Hyderabad with immediate access to a fully equipped, medically certified ambulance — ensuring that the journey from emergency to treatment is fast, safe, and seamlessly coordinated with receiving hospitals.

Our Vision

An Advanced Emergency Response Network Across Hyderabad

To establish the most comprehensive, technology-integrated emergency medical transport network in Hyderabad — a system where no patient is more than 10 minutes from a dispatched ambulance, and where clinical care begins the moment we arrive.
